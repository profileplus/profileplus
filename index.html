[ 01 ] — ABOUT THE ACADEMY

A decade of
turning silence
into conviction.

↳ Founded 2010 by Padma Sundar & Mr. Sundar

Profile Plus is a Training Academy in Coimbatore. The main aim is to equip our students with the basic skills required to make English their first language — to read it, write it, and live it.

What began in 2010 as an idea between two educators has become a small, fiercely personal academy. We sit beside our students. We listen to the tangle in their sentences. And we untangle it — patiently, professionally, persistently — until the world’s language sounds like their own.

// Vision

To develop personality professionally, and the skills that future leaders need to face a real-time world. We believe everyone has stupendous power and adroitness.

// Mission

To cultivate the potential in every individual to thrive as a global citizen — by fostering a love for learning and civic engagement to achieve great knowledge.

// Goals

To transform every individual to feel confident with the global language, and to groom them to face the world on their own terms.

[ 02 ] — METHODOLOGY

One person.
One curriculum.
One conversation at a time.

Profile Plus is proficient in over a decade of English language training, delivered one-on-one. Our study material is detailed, interesting and easy to understand — and our sessions are unhurried by design.

01 DIAGNOSE

Listen first, prescribe second.

A short conversation tells us where the tangle is — grammar, hesitation, fear of speaking, accent confidence. The plan is shaped around that, not a textbook.

02 DRILL

Practice that feels like a conversation.

Mini-debates, role-plays, short presentations, applied grammar drills — practice that is rigorous but never stiff. Mistakes happen here, not in the world outside.

03 DEPLOY

From the classroom into the boardroom.

Interview practice, resume drafting, public speaking, lateral thinking — every learner leaves with the toolkit to walk into a room and own it.
